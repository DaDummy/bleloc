\heading{BleLoc Bluetooth Device Tracker}

\begin{flushleft}
	BleLoc is an App that help you find missing Bluetooth devices.
	
	You can add any of your paired Bluetooth devices. 
	
	The app then tracks if anyone has seen it and shows you the locations on a map.
\end{flushleft}
\vspace{5mm}
\heading{What to do}

\begin{flushleft}
	Please take a couple of minutes to familiarize yourself with the app.
	
	Try to use it with just the description above in mind, as you would any app that you've just installed.
	
	After that, please answer a couple of questions.
\end{flushleft}
\vspace{5mm}
